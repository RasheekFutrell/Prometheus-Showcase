Orion Insights
💡 The Rule of 72
Divide 72 by your annual return to find how long it takes money to double. At 7%, your money doubles in ~10.3 years. At 10%, just 7.2 years. This is why rate of return matters more than most people realise.
📐 Debt-to-Income Ratio
DTI = total monthly debt payments ÷ gross monthly income. Lenders want below 36%. Below 20% puts you in excellent standing. Above 43% and most mortgage lenders will decline you regardless of credit score.
🎯 The 4% Withdrawal Rule
You can withdraw 4% of your portfolio annually without running out of money over 30 years. Multiply your target annual spending by 25 to get your FIRE number. Spend $48,000/year? You need $1.2M invested.
🏦 Tax-Advantaged Account Order
Always capture the full 401(k) employer match first — it's a guaranteed 50–100% return on day one. Then HSA if eligible. Then IRA. Then back to 401(k). Taxable brokerage last.
